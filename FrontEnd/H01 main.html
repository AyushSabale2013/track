  Our Inspiration

Shree Jyotirling Shikshan Sanstha Talsande has established in 2004, a tiny village, Talsande on the Wathar - Warananagar road. The main objective of the Institute is to impart qualify professional education to the students from rural area. The students passing out from the Institute are expected to generate suitable source of income and uplift their family’s economic status. This in turn will reflect in the rural upheld went of the surrounding areas.

"Great things never came from comfort zones."

       SHREE ANNASO PATIL
       (Founder of Shamrao Patil Shikshan Samuh)
