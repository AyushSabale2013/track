Book Your Journey to IIT with JEE Preparation

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is India’s premier engineering entrance assessment, comprising two stages: JEE Main and JEE Advanced. It serves as the gateway to elite institutions like IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs.

The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) manages the admission process for 23 IITs, 31 NITs, 25 IIITs, and other government-funded institutes. Additionally, institutes such as IISc, IISER, and IIST use JEE Advanced scores, though they follow a separate counseling process.

Once admitted to an IIT, candidates are no longer eligible to reappear for JEE Advanced.

Track Academy is a leading coaching institute in the Kolhapur district, offering classroom and distance learning programs for JEE Main, JEE Advanced, Pre-Medical, and Foundation Courses. With expert faculty, online resources, mock tests, and high-quality video lectures, Track Academy is dedicated to shaping student success.

🔍 Why Track Academy?

📚 The Track Academy Course is specially designed for Class 12th passed students who are aspiring for elite technical institutions such as the National Institutes of Technology (NITs). This program is ideal for students who don’t want to invest 4 years in an average engineering college — instead, they possess the confidence and determination to retake JEE Main for a rank they truly deserve.
