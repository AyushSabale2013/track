Book Your Journey to AIIMS with NEET Preparation

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is India’s central medical entrance examination, conducted for admission to undergraduate courses in MBBS, BDS, BAMS, and other medical programs in government and private institutions across the country.

NEET is administered by the National Testing Agency (NTA), and its scores are used for seat allocation through centralized counseling conducted by bodies such as MCC for All India Quota and various state authorities for state quotas.

Institutes like AIIMS and JIPMER also admit students based on NEET scores, and no other entrance exams are accepted for undergraduate medical courses in India.

Track Academy is a reputed coaching institute in the Kolhapur district, offering focused preparation for NEET, JEE, Pre-Foundation, and Foundation Courses. With experienced faculty, comprehensive study material, regular mock tests, and personalized mentoring, Track Academy helps students achieve their medical career goals.
