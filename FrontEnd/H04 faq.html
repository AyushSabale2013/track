JEE/NEET Academy - Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the difference between JEE and NEET?

JEE is for engineering aspirants (IITs, NITs), while NEET is for medical aspirants (MBBS, BDS). Both are national-level exams with different syllabi and approaches.

Q2. What classes or grades can join your academy?

We have batches for Class 8-10 (Foundation), Class 11-12 (Target), and Repeaters for both JEE & NEET.

Q3. Is the coaching provided online, offline, or hybrid?

We offer all three modes: Offline, Online (live & recorded), and Hybrid for flexibility and effectiveness.

Q4. What subjects are covered for JEE coaching?

Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry are thoroughly covered with NCERT, PYQs, and advanced problem-solving.

Q5. Do you prepare students for both JEE Main and Advanced?

Yes. Our modules are tailored to prepare students for both levels with appropriate difficulty and strategy.

Q6. How often are mock tests conducted?

Weekly tests, monthly evaluations, and full-syllabus mocks with analytics and feedback are provided regularly.

Q7. What subjects are taught for NEET preparation?

Biology (Botany + Zoology), Physics, and Chemistry are taught based on NCERT and supplemented with MCQs and mock tests.

Q8. Is NCERT enough for NEET?

NCERT is essential, especially for Biology. However, for top ranks, additional materials and problem practice are provided.

Q9. Do you provide support for NEET UG counselling as well?

Yes, expert mentors guide students in college selection, counselling, and form filling after NEET results.

Q10. Are study materials and doubt sessions included in the fee?

Yes. Students receive curated notes, NCERT boosters, formula booklets, and regular doubt-clearing support.

Q11. What is the batch strength and student-teacher ratio?

We keep batch sizes small (30-50 students) for personal attention and better academic tracking.

Q12. Are parents updated on student performance?

Yes, with monthly reports, PTMs, and app-based notifications for tests, attendance, and performance.

Q13. Do you offer scholarships or fee waivers?

Yes. Scholarships are based on our entrance test, board results, or financial need. Apply to know your eligibility.
